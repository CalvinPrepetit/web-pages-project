The Ramos-Ibrahim Family

Meet the Ramos-Ibrahim family, a modern fictional family of five in Charlotte, North Carolina, blending Puerto Rican, Egyptian, and African American roots with a lifestyle built around community, travel, sports, arts, and trying something new together.

Family Members

Child 2

Child 3

Last Family Vacation

Last summer the Ramos-Ibrahim family spent eight days in Costa Rica. They wanted a trip that felt active, educational, and memorable, so they mixed outdoor adventure with time to learn about the country's wildlife, food, and culture.

  • Leila filmed the trip and turned it into a travel video journal.
  • Mateo loved zip-lining through the rainforest and kayaking near the coast.
  • Nadia talked nonstop about the hanging bridges and wildlife rescue center.
  • Sofia signed the family up for a cooking class with local dishes.
  • Omar mapped out waterfall hikes and sunrise photo stops along the route.

Why They Enjoy Time Together

Even with school, work, and busy schedules, the Ramos-Ibrahim family makes time for shared experiences. They are the kind of family that signs up for community events, invites friends over, explores new neighborhoods, and turns ordinary weekends into something memorable.

Their story is fictional, but it reflects a modern family that is open to different cultures, deeply involved in their community, and always ready for the next adventure.
